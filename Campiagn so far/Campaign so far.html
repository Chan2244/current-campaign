Welcome to the Cilica Civil War Campaign! the Cilica Civil War is a Pathfinder campaign set in the world of Vakalin, the King named King Valieric and has an advisor named The Hidden One who wears all black including an onyx black mask, the Prince is named Corlin, who has fled to the saftey of the sacred sands desert, the prince is outlawed as he is in open rebellion with his father, the king. The prince has a group of loyal followers who are trying to help him reclaim his throne the king is now looking for mercinarys to strike at the prince. Our adventuers have made it to the town of Hearthfire, where they are attacked and betrayed by a group of mercinarys working for the king, two of the adventurers are killed and the third found two prisoners in one of the fortress cells one of them a human fighter, the other a wizard. The adventurer frees the two prisoners and they escape together, the three of them are now on the run from the king's forces and are trying to find a way to survive in this dangerous world. As they travel, they will encounter various factions and characters, some of whom may be allies, while others may be enemies. The adventurers will have to navigate the complex political landscape of the Cilica Civil War, making choices that will affect the outcome of the campaign. Will they side with the prince and his loyal followers, or will they join forces with the king and his mercinarys? The fate of Cilica hangs in the balance, and it's up to the adventurers to decide which path they will take. The campaign is filled with action, intrigue, and adventure, and promises to be an exciting journey for all involved.
